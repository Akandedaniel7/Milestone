About BDEL

Black Diamond Engineering Limited is a Nigerian based Civil and Structural Engineering company that offers the most effective and reliable building services in the country. We are a vast well experienced company that boasts of handling state of the art projects and executing them with speed, efficiency and precision.

When it comes to handling projects of a large nature, we are not ones to shy away or under perform. With a well trained staff of professionals that carry out their duties with pride and diligence, we are certain of accomplishing any goal we set our sights on.

When everything is taken into account, the best part of handling projects are the feedbacks we get from our clients. We are on a winning streak of 100% positive feedback by all our clients so far. Being perfectionist and overachievers, this record brings us great joy and sense of pride and we don’t plan on making it waiver.
